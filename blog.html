About Us

Shree Marwadi Vyayamshala, established and managed by a trusted organization, has been serving the community of Bhagalpur for many years. Our mission is to promote physical fitness, mental well-being, and traditional Indian sports among the residents of Bhagalpur.

We take pride in offering a comprehensive range of fitness facilities including a modern gym, yoga classes, table tennis, badminton, and mallakhamb. Our dedicated team of trained professionals ensures that members receive proper guidance and support in their fitness journey.

Our yoga classes are conducted following the authentic methodology and teachings of the prestigious Bihar School of Yoga, Munger. This ensures that our members receive genuine yogic education rooted in traditional practices while incorporating modern understanding of health and wellness.
